## Meta Analysis

### Strengths
1. **Deathwing -1D** — massive durability boost
2. **Deep Strike** — deploy anywhere turn 2
3. **Ravenwing mobility** — Fly + high movement
4. **Character auras** — Azrael 4+ invuln is huge

### Weaknesses
1. **Expensive elites** — few units, few objectives
2. **Melee-focused Deathwing** — need to get close
3. **No cheap battleline** — Intercessors are 80pts
4. **Limited anti-tank** — relies on Gladiator/Redemptor

### Meta Position
- **Tier 1** in Purge the Foe (Deathwing -1D dominates)
- **Tier 2** in Take and Hold (expensive units = risky)
- **Tier 1** in Reconnaissance (Ravenwing mobility)
- **Tier 2** in Priority Assets (needs more units)
- **Tier 1** in Disruption (balanced stats)

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Sammael is the best all-rounder** — cheap, mobile, durable (105pts, DPP 0.115)
2. **Deathwing Command Squad is the best DPP** — 0.1345 at 110pts
3. **Ravenwing wins mobility missions** — Fly is huge
4. **Lion El'Jonson is premium but expensive** — 285pts limits army building
5. **Deathwing Knights at 240pts are a risky anchor** — rank 15 in Take and Hold
