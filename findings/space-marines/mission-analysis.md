# Space Marines — Mission Analysis

DPP engine output for Space Marines across all 5 dispositions.

---

## DPP Rankings per Disposition

### 🟢 PURGE THE FOE (Kill-heavy)

**Best detachments:** Ironstorm Spearhead (2DP), Firestorm Assault Force (2DP)

**Top DPP units vs MEQ:**

| Unit | Pts | DPP | SURV Turns | Why |
|------|----:|----:|-----------:|-----|
| Dreadnought | 135 | **0.0919** | 1.0 | Multi-melta, high S, good AP |
| Captain In Terminator Armour | 85 | 0.0888 | 1.2 | Power fist + storm bolter |
| Devastator Squad | 120 | 0.0722 | 1.0 | Lascannon/melta platform |
| Brutalis Dreadnought | 150 | 0.0716 | 1.4 | Twin multi-melta + melee |

**Verdict:** 🟢 **S-tier.** Dreadnoughts are the DPP kings. Multi-melta (S10 AP-4 D6) into MEQ gives 0.0919 DPP — highest in the faction.

### 🟢 PRIORITY ASSETS (Protect key units)

**Best detachments:** Gladius Task Force (3DP), Hammer of Avernii (2DP)

**Analysis:**
- Captain In Terminator Armour (0.0888 DPP) is the value king
- Dreadnoughts (0.0919 DPP) are the damage kings
- Terminators (2.2 turns SURV) are the durability kings

**Verdict:** 🟢 **A-tier.** Oath of Moment +1 to hit on key units is strong. Protect the Dreadnoughts and Terminators.

### 🟡 TAKE AND HOLD (Objective play)

**Best detachments:** Ceramite Sentinels (3DP), Anvil Siege Force (2DP)

**Analysis:**
- Dreadnoughts (OC 3) are decent objective holders
- Land Raider (OC 5) is the best objective holder
- Terminators (OC 1) are bad at holding objectives

**Verdict:** 🟡 **B-tier.** SM have decent OC on vehicles but lack cheap objective holders. Land Raider is great but expensive.

### 🟠 RECONNAISSANCE (Action-heavy)

**Best detachments:** Vanguard Spearhead (2DP), Librarius Conclave (1DP)

**Analysis:**
- SM want to push and kill, not sit doing actions
- No cheap action monkeys in the faction
- Terminators are too expensive for actions

**Verdict:** 🟠 **C-tier.** SM lack the cheap units needed for action-heavy playstyles.

### 🔴 DISRUPTION (Screening/denial)

**Best detachments:** Stormlance Task Force (3DP), Shadowmark Talon (2DP)

**Analysis:**
- SM lack cheap screening units compared to GK (80pt Strike Squads)
- No infiltrate/scout units in the core army
- Disruption requires cheap, expendable units

**Verdict:** 🔴 **D-tier.** SM are too expensive and elite for screening/denial play.

## Unit Efficiency Analysis

### DPP per Point (Value)

| Unit | Pts | DPP | DPP/kpt |
|------|----:|----:|--------:|
| Captain In Terminator Armour | 85 | 0.0888 | **1.04** |
| Librarian | 60 | 0.0557 | 0.93 |
| Captain | 80 | 0.0659 | 0.82 |
| Dreadnought | 135 | 0.0919 | 0.68 |
| Devastator Squad | 120 | 0.0722 | 0.60 |

**Key insight:** Captain In Terminator Armour is the best value unit in the faction (1.04 DPP/kpt). Characters are efficient because they're cheap and have good melee.

### SURV vs DPP Trade-off

| Unit | Pts | DPP | SURV Turns | DPP×SURV |
|------|----:|----:|-----------:|---------:|
| Dreadnought | 135 | 0.0919 | 1.0 | 0.0919 |
| Captain In Terminator Armour | 85 | 0.0888 | 1.2 | 0.1066 |
| Brutalis Dreadnought | 150 | 0.0716 | 1.4 | 0.1002 |
| Land Raider | 220 | 0.0597 | 1.9 | 0.1134 |
| Terminator Squad | 160 | 0.0360 | 2.2 | 0.0792 |

**Key insight:** Land Raider has the highest DPP×SURV product (0.1134) — it's the most efficient unit when considering both damage and survivability.

## Detachment Modifier Impact

### Ironstorm Spearhead (Purge the Foe)
- **Armoured Wrath:** Vehicle reroll 1s to hit
- Impact: +15% DPP on Dreadnoughts (0.0919 → 0.1057)

### Firestorm Assault Force (Purge the Foe)
- **Close-Range Eradication:** +1 AP within 12"
- Impact: +20% DPP on short-range weapons (Melta, Plasma)

### Vanguard Spearhead (Reconnaissance)
- **Shadow Masters:** Stealth on Phobos units
- Impact: +10% SURV on Phobos units

## Key Findings

1. **Dreadnoughts are the DPP kings** — Multi-melta loadout gives 0.0919 DPP vs MEQ
2. **Terminators are the SURV wall** — 2.2 turns, best in the faction
3. **Land Raider is the best all-rounder** — 0.0597 DPP + 1.9 SURV = 0.1134 DPP×SURV
4. **Characters are the best value** — Captain In Terminator Armour: 1.04 DPP/kpt
5. **SM lack cheap screening units** — Disruption is D-tier
6. **Oath of Moment +1 to hit is the best army-wide buff** — every unit benefits
7. **SM have 5+ invuln on Terminators** — not 4+ like Grey Knights
8. **No native FNP on any SM unit** — unlike Grey Knights' Rhino FNP 6+ vs mortals

## Assumptions

- Target: MEQ (T4 SV3+) unless otherwise noted
- No cover factored into saves
- No detachment buffs, stratagems, or command rerolls
- No unit coherency or transport constraints
- Average dice (no variance band)
- Oath of Moment +1 to hit assumed on all attacks
