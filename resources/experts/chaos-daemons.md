# Chaos Daemons — 11th Edition Expert

Chaos Daemons are warp entities of the four Chaos Gods (Khorne, Tzeentch, Nurgle,
Slaanesh) plus Undivided. Army strengths: Deep Strike across whole faction,
god-specific specialization, 4++ invuln natively, and Shadow of Chaos board control.

**Key characteristics:**
- All units have 4++ invuln (some 5++)
- All units have Deep Strike natively
- No shooting for most — primarily melee + psychic
- Split into 4 mono-god sub-factions + Undivided (Be'lakor)
- Shadow of Chaos is the core army rule: conditional board-wide aura

---

## Army Rules

### The Shadow of Chaos
If your Army Faction is Legiones Daemonica, certain areas are in your Shadow of Chaos:
- **Your deployment zone:** always
- **No Man's Land:** if you control ≥ half objectives in NML (start of phase, until end)
- **Enemy deployment zone:** if you control ≥ half objectives in enemy DZ (start of phase, until end)

### Daemonic Manifestation
While a LEGIONES DAEMONICA unit is within your army's Shadow of Chaos:
- Each time it takes a Battle-shock test: +1 to the test
- If test is passed: one model regains up to D3 lost wounds
- If Battleline unit and test passed: up to D3 destroyed models returned instead

### Daemonic Terror
While an enemy unit is within your army's Shadow of Chaos AND/OR within 6" of a
named Greater Daemon (Bloodthirster, Great Unclean One, Kairos, Keeper, Lord of
Change, Rotigus, Shalaxi Helbane, Skarbrand):
- Each time it takes a Battle-shock test: -1 to that test
- If test is failed: that enemy unit suffers D3 mortal wounds

### Daemonic Pact (Allies)
If every model in your army has the CHAOS KNIGHTS or HERETIC ASTARTES keyword,
can include LEGIONES DAEMONICA units:
- Incursion: up to 250pts
- Strike Force: up to 500pts
- Onslaught: up to 750pts
- Cannot be Warlord, cannot get Enhancements
- Battleline tax: non-Battleline per god ≤ Battleline per god

**Critical:** Allied Daemons do NOT get Shadow of Chaos — gated on "If your Army
Faction is Legiones Daemonica". They keep CORE abilities + individual datasheet
abilities only.

## Detachments

Daemons have 9 detachments in 11e. DP budget: typically 2-3 DP at 2000pts.

### Daemonic Incursion (1DP)
**Rule — Warp Rifts:** When a unit sets up via Deep Strike wholly within Shadow of
Chaos and/or within 6" of a matching-god Greater Daemon, it can come in >6"
instead of >9".
**Stratagems:** Corrupt Realspace (1CP), Warp Surge (1CP), Draught of Terror (1CP),
Denizens of the Warp (1CP), The Realm of Chaos (1CP), Daemonic Invulnerability (1CP)
**Enhancements:** A'rgath (Khorne, +A/S melee), The Everstave (Tzeentch, +S/R),
The Endless Gift (Nurgle, FNP5+), Soulstealer (Slaanesh, regen on kill)

### Shadow Legion (Be'lakor — 1DP)
**Restriction:** Cannot include Daemon Prince, Daemon Prince with Wings, or any
other Epic Heroes except Be'lakor.
**Rule — First Prince of Chaos:** Units gain god-specific keywords and rules:
- Khorne: Murderer's Cowl (Advance/charge/shoot)
- Tzeentch: Penumbral Puppetry (Stealth, -1 hit in melee)
- Nurgle: Gloam Rot (S > T = -1 to wound)
- Slaanesh: Shadow's Caress (can't be snap shot)
- Undivided: Dark Pacts, Deep Strike for HA units
**Allies:** Can include CSM Heretic Astartes units (Legionaries, Chosen, Possessed,
Terminators, etc.) up to 500/1000/1500pts per battle size.
**Stratagems:** Spiteful Demise (1CP), Channelled Wrath (1CP), Death Denied (1CP),
Encroaching Darkness (1CP), Shade Path (2CP), Binding Shadow (1CP)
**Enhancements:** Leaping Shadows (Scouts 9"), Mantle of Gloom (Aura, -1 OC),
Fade to Darkness (SR after fight), Malice Made Manifest (mortals in fight)

### Cavalcade of Chaos (Mounted focus — 1DP)
**Rule — Unholy Avalanche:** MOUNTED units that Fall Back can still shoot/charge.
**Enhancements:** Apocalyptic Steeds (+1" M), Soul-Shattering Charge (pile-in 3")
**Stratagems:** From Beyond the Veil (1CP, ingress), Warp-Riders (1CP, Mobile),
Inescapable Manifestations (1CP, desperate escape on fall back)

### Lords of the Warp (Character focus — 1DP)
**Rule — Loci of Power:** CHARACTER non-MONSTER models have +1 LD and +1 OC.
**Enhancements:** Swollen With Power (+2 W)
**Stratagems:** Carnival of Excess (1CP, Fights First Slaanesh character),

## Key Modeling Notes

1. **Daemonic Pact allies do NOT get Shadow of Chaos** — the army rule is gated
   on "If your Army Faction is Legiones Daemonica". Allied daemons in a CK/CSM
   army keep CORE abilities and individual datasheet abilities only. They do not
   benefit from Daemonic Manifestation, Daemonic Terror, or any detachment rule.

2. **Be'lakor IS Legiones Daemonica** — when taken as a CK ally, he keeps his
   datasheet abilities (The Dark Master aura extends Shadow of Chaos 6",
   Shadow Form selection) but the Daemon army rule Shadow of Chaos only covers
   the default areas (DZ, NML with control, enemy DZ with control — all gated
   on "If your Army Faction is Legiones Daemonica", which fails in CK/CSM).

3. **Horrors Split is not recursion** — Split is a "when destroyed" trigger, not
   a "spawn new unit". Models added can exceed starting strength. When no Pink
   Horrors remain, switch to Blue Horrors datasheet. Cannot return Pink Horrors
   to a Blue Horror unit via any method.

4. **Soul Grinder has no native god keyword** — must select Daemonic Allegiance
   on army selection. This determines its additional weapon and keyword.

5. **Daemon Prince has Lone Operative when near Infantry** — "Daemonic Lord":
   while within 3" of friendly Legiones Daemonica Infantry, has Lone Operative.

6. **Greater Daemon auras extend Shadow of Chaos** — each god's Greater Daemon
   (Bloodthirster, Lord of Change, Great Unclean One, Keeper of Secrets) has
   a 6" aura: "While a friendly X Legiones Daemonica unit is within 6" of this
   model, that unit is within your army's Shadow of Chaos." This is critical
   for expanding Shadow of Chaos beyond objective control.

7. **Skarbrand gives +1 A melee** to Khorne units within 6" (Rage Embodied aura).
   This compounds with Bloodthirster's +1 to hit (Daemon Lord of Khorne).

8. **Faction Pack datasheets are authoritative** — all weapon profiles above
   are from the 11e FP PDF, not BSData 10e.

9. **No T1 Deep Strike** — standard Reserves restriction. Affects mobility
   scoring on deployment-heavy missions.

10. **Fortifications (Skull Altar, Feculent Gnarlmaw)** — provide SoC extension
    and Cover. Skull Altar gives SoC within 6". Gnarlmaw gives Stealth to
    Nurgle units within 6". Both provide Cover vs ranged through obscured.

## Gotchas

### Shadow of Chaos area depends on board control
Your DZ is always SoC. NML and enemy DZ require objective control thresholds.
This makes SoC unreliable early game against a spread-out opponent.

### Greater Daemon keyword requirements for Warp Rifts
Daemonic Incursion's "Denizens of the Warp" lets Deep Striking units come in
>6" if wholly within SoC AND/OR within 6" of a matching-god Greater Daemon.
The Greater Daemon must share the Khorne/Tzeentch/Nurgle/Slaanesh keyword
with the arriving unit.

### Be'lakor Supreme Commander
Be'lakor MUST be your Warlord if in the army. Cannot be taken as a non-Warlord
in a Daemons army, but as a CK ally he is not your Warlord anyway (allied
Daemons cannot be Warlord per Daemonic Pact).

### Flux tokens are shared resources
Scintillating Legion's Fates in Flux gives your opponent your Flux tokens.
Spending discipline is critical — don't give the opponent re-rolls carelessly.

### Plague Legion's Melancholic Miasma vs Daemonic Terror
Plague Legion's detachment rule forces an enemy unit to take a Battle-shock
test each Command phase. If they fail, Daemonic Terror triggers D3 mortals
(if they're also within 6" of a named Greater Daemon). The Cankerblight
enhancement explicitly says "no mortal wounds are inflicted by your army's
Daemonic Terror rule" when used — it replaces the Terror mortals with
its own destroy-one-model effect.

### Legion of Excess Seductive Gambit trades Fights First
This is a meaningful trade — Daemonettes and characters have native Fights
First. Opting into Seductive Gambit loses that for re-rolls. Choose per charge.

### Soul Grinder Scuttling Walker
Can move over friendly Monster/Vehicle models and terrain ≤4" height freely.
This is a mobility advantage not reflected in base MOB scoring.
