# Chaos Knights — 11th Edition Expert

The Chaos Knights are a faction of corrupted war engines, bound to daemonic entities
and driven by unholy purpose. Army strengths: high-T multi-wound stat blocks,
Sustained Hits melee, flexible War Dog screening, and daemon ally options.

---

## Army Rules

### Harbingers of Dread
When a CHAOS KNIGHTS unit from your army causes a battleshock test, the Darkness
counts as 1 higher (e.g. 6" battleshock aura becomes 7", or -1 to Combat Attrition
tests becomes -2). This is NOT a -1 to hit modifier — it affects battleshock only.

**Mechanical effect:** Stealth (from Darkness). NOT "Stealth" in the keyword sense —
it's the darkness aura making ranged attacks harder. The engine does NOT model this
automatically.

### Super-heavy Walker
CHAOS KNIGHTS units with the WALKER keyword can move over models and terrain freely.
This provides no direct DPP benefit but affects board positioning.

## Melee Weapon Rules (11e)

### Reaper chaintalon (War Dog) [Sustained Hits 1]
12A, S8, AP-2, D1. This is the "sweep" equivalent. Note: the War Dog datasheet
does NOT separate strike/sweep for its Reaper chaintalon — it's always 12 attacks.

### Reaper chaintalon (Questoris) [strike/sweep]
- Strike: 4A, S10, AP-3, D3
- Sweep: 8A, S8, AP-2, D1

### Warpstrike claw [strike/sweep]
- Strike: 4A, S20, AP-3, D8 — anti-tank crack
- Sweep: 8A, S10, AP-2, D3 — elite/heavy infantry

### Slaughterclaw (War Dog) [Sustained Hits 1]
6A, S12, AP-3, D5.5. Heavy hitter. Combined with Reaper chaintalon (12A),
the Karnivore puts out 18 melee attacks with Sustained Hits 1.

### Balemace [Extra Attacks]
3A, S8, AP-1, D2. As an Extra Attacks weapon, it is ALWAYS in addition to
the best non-EA melee choice (Electroscourge at 9A). So Abominant effectively
has: Electroscourge (9A, S10, AP-2, D3, Sustained Hits 1) + Balemace (3A,
S8, AP-1, D2).

### Cerastus shock lance [Assault, Sustained Hits 2]
Ranged: 6A, S6, AP0, D2, Sustained Hits 2.
Melee - strike: 4A, S16, AP-4, D8, Lance.
Melee - sweep: 12A, S8, AP-2, D2.

The ranged profile is notable: 6 shots, Sustained Hits 2 (avg 2 extra hits),
Assault (can advance and fire). The Lancer keeps 14" M.

## Army Rule Interactions

### Harbingers of Dread (Darkness)
Apply Stealth-like effect (enemy -1 to ranged hit rolls against CK).
The engine does NOT auto-apply this. When computing SURV for CK units
under Harbingers of Dread, apply `hit_mod = -1` to incoming ranged attacks.

### Super-heavy Walker
All CHAOS KNIGHTS VEHICLE units ignore terrain penalties for movement.
No direct DPP effect.

## Detachments

CK have 8 detachments in 11e. Total DP budget is typically 2-3DP.

### Bastions Of Tyranny (1DP)
**Restriction:** TYRANT-class only.
Focuses on the Knight Tyrant with buffs specific to Dominus chassis.

### Hunting Warpack (1DP)
**Restriction:** WAR DOG only.
Hybrid ranged/melee detachment for War Dogs.

### Iconoclast Fiefdom (1DP)
Allows DAMNED keyword allies from CSM (Cultists, Traitor Guardsmen, etc.)
up to 500pt (Incursion) or 1000pt (Strike Force) or 1500pt (Onslaught).
DAMNED allies get leadership re-roll and become targets for detachment
auras/stratagems.

**Critical:** DAMNED allies do NOT get Dark Pacts — gated on "If your Army
Faction is Heretic Astartes". CK army faction = CHAOS KNIGHTS, condition
fails. They keep CORE abilities and individual datasheet abilities only.

### Helhunt Lance (2DP)
All-around detachment. Synergy between War Dogs and big knights.

### Houndpack Lance (2DP)
**Restriction:** WAR DOG only.
Buffs for War Dog units in packs.

### Lords Of Dread (2DP)
Objective play detachment. Battleshock synergy, board control.

### Traitoris Lance (2DP)
Purge detachment. Kill-focused buffs.

### Infernal Lance (3DP)
**Detachment Rule — Malefic Surge**
In your Command phase, one or more CHAOS KNIGHTS units can make a Malefic
Surge. Each that does takes a Leadership test; if failed, suffer D3 mortal
wounds. Until your next Command phase, the unit is **Empowered**.

When an Empowered unit is activated (selected to move/shoot/fight/targeted),
choose ONE:
- **Unholy Hunger:** +3" movement when selected to Move
- **Diabolic Power:** [LETHAL HITS] or [SUSTAINED HITS 1] when selected to
  Shoot or Fight
- **Unnatural Fortitude:** 5+ invuln or 6+ FNP when targeted by an attack

**Note:** At 3DP, Infernal Lance takes the entire DP budget (max 3DP).
Cannot be combined with any other detachment.

**DP costs:**

| Iconoclast Fiefdom | 1 |
| Helhunt Lance | 2 |
| Houndpack Lance | 2 |

## Ally Rules

### Daemons (Daemonic Pact)
- Up to 500pt in Strike Force (2000pt game)
- Any unit with CHAOS KNIGHTS or HERETIC ASTARTES keyword (on all models)
  can include LEGIONES DAEMONICA units
- Battleline tax: # of non-Battleline per god ≤ # of Battleline per god
- Allied Daemon units cannot be Warlord or take Enhancements

**Do NOT get:**
- Harbingers of Dread (CK army rule)
- The Shadow of Chaos (Daemons army rule — gated on "If your Army Faction
  is Legiones Daemonica")

**Keep:**
- CORE abilities (Deep Strike, Feel No Pain, etc.)
- Individual datasheet abilities (e.g. Daemon Prince's "Daemonic Lord",
  Karanak's "Prey of the Blood God", Be'lakor's own battleshock rules)

### DAMNED (Iconoclast Fiefdom)
- Max 500pt (Incursion) / 1000pt (Strike Force) / 1500pt (Onslaught)
- DAMNED keyword units from CSM: Cultists, Traitor Guardsmen
- Not all DAMNED units are valid — Gellerpox, Accursed Cultists, etc.

**Do NOT get:**
- Harbingers of Dread (CK army rule)
- Dark Pacts (CSM army rule — gated on "If your Army Faction is Heretic
  Astartes")

**Keep:**
- CORE abilities
- Individual datasheet abilities

## Key Modeling Notes

1. **Despoiler modelled with 2× gatling cannon** — the MEQ spam build.
   Alternative: 2× lascutter (anti-tank) or 1+1. The 2× gatling gives
   the highest DPP vs MEQ/light vehicle and is the meta pick.

2. **War Dog Moirax has a flexible weapon loadout** — the engine evaluates
   all weapons per target profile, not per model. In practice the Moirax
   picks different weapons for different targets.

3. **Karnivore is pure melee** — minimal ranged (stubber + multi-launcher).
   Its value is entirely in the Reaper chaintalon + Slaughterclaw combo
   (18A sustained melee).

4. **Cerastus Lancer is the fastest knight** at 14" M with Assault on its
   shock lance — can advance and still shoot.

5. **Tyrant is a gun platform** — 7 ranged weapons, but moves only 8".
   It wins DPP by volume, not efficiency.

6. **Acastus knights exist in a different weight class** — T13, 2+, 30W.
   Their DPP is terrible due to cost (725-785pts), but they're the
   toughest units in the faction. Porphyrion is the "better" Acastus
   (same T/SV/W, lower pts, better weapons).

7. **Imperial Knights ally rule**: CK cannot ally with Imperial Knights.

8. **No native invuln on War Dogs or most Questoris** — War Dogs have no
   invuln natively. Some big knights have 4++ (Desecrator, Rampager,
   Lancer, Porphyrion). In Infernal Lance, Empowered units can gain 5++
   or 6+ FNP.

9. **Daemon allies as secondary action monkeys** — Nurglings (~40pts) for
   screening and actions. Plaguebearers for Battleline tax (~110pts/10).

## Gotchas

### Despoiler has flexible arm weapons
The Despoiler can take any 2 arm weapons. Common builds:
- 2× gatling cannon (MEQ/light vehicle spam)
- 2× lascutter (anti-tank)
- 1× gatling + 1× lascutter (balanced)
The engine currently models 2× gatling. To evaluate other builds,
change `vehicles.json` for Knight Despoiler.

### Weapon options are evaluated per target
The engine evaluates ALL weapons for a unit against a target profile and
sums damage. For the Moirax, this means ALL 5+ weapons fire. This is
correct — the Moirax has a fixed loadout in the config (all weapons).

### No T1 Deep Strike
Reserves cannot arrive on turn 1. This affects mobility scoring for
Deep Strike units on deployment-heavy missions.

### Faction Pack weapon stats may differ from BSData
The 11e Faction Pack PDF may update weapon profiles (values shown above
are from BSData 10e). If re-extracted from the FP PDF, verify values.

### Infernal Lance is 3DP — no combining
At 2000pts with 3DP budget, Infernal Lance takes the entire allocation.
Cannot pair with any other detachment.

### Cerastus vs Questoris chassis differences
Cerastus: T11, 28W, 12-14" M
Questoris: T11, 26W, 10-12" M
Tyrant: T12, 28W, 8" M (Dominus)
Acastus: T13, 30W, 8" M

### War Dogs lack WALKER keyword in data
In the BSData, War Dogs are VEHICLE without WALKER. This means they
don't benefit from some WALKER-specific rules in the engine. If the
11e datasheets add WALKER, update `get_unit_info` in `ranking.py`.

### Melee DPP assumes charge reaches target
The engine sums ranged + melee DPP. In practice, melee-only units
(Karnivore, Rampager) need to reach combat first. The raw DPP value
is theoretical — real output depends on delivery.

## Disposition Ranking (best to worst)

| Tier | Disposition | Why |
|------|-------------|-----|
| 🟢 **S** | **Purge the Foe** | CK kills things. Every unit contributes. Natural fit. |
| 🟢 **S** | **Take and Hold** | With Iconoclast cheap bodies hold objectives. Without: tough Knights still work. |
| 🟡 **A** | **Priority Assets** | Protect key Knights. OK but CK wants to push, not castle. |
| 🟠 **B** | **Disruption** | Screening/control. Low model count struggles here. |
| 🔴 **C** | **Reconnaissance** | Action-heavy. Worst for CK — big bases, few units, can't do actions effectively. |

**Key constraint:** Actions are bad in 11th edition, per user. Purge and Take and Hold are the only viable competitive picks.

## Top Competitive Combinations (3DP at 2000pts)

### 🟢 S-Tier — Tournament level

1. **Infernal Lance** (3DP, Purge). Best single detachment. Self-contained. Run Purge.
2. **Houndpack Lance + Iconoclast Fiefdom** (2+1, TaH). Best rules package. Marked Prey + cheap bodies. 12-18 War Dogs + Cultists. Pick TaH.

### 🟡 A-Tier — Very good

3. **Traitoris Lance + Iconoclast Fiefdom** (2+1, Purge/TaH). Two S-tier dispositions. Pick Purge vs gunlines, TaH vs horde. Weak rules but flexible.
4. **Traitoris Lance + Bastions of Tyranny** (2+1, Purge). Purge + Tyrant buff. Weak rules but efficient DP spend.
5. **Lords of Dread + Iconoclast Fiefdom** (2+1, TaH). Lords is mediocre but Iconoclast carries. +2 OC helps hold.

### Key Rule

**Iconoclast Fiefdom is in every S/A tier combo.** It fixes CK's only real weakness — cheap objective bodies. Any list without it has a harder path to win.
