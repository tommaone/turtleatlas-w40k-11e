# Meta Bias & Mission Priority

Source: Mordian Glory (community consensus, July 2026).

## Core Claim

In 11th Edition, only **two mission types** are competitively viable:

| Mission | Weight | Why |
|---------|--------|-----|
| **Purge the Foe** | Primary | Kill-focused — rewards high DPS, trading efficiency, threat range |
| **Take and Hold** | Secondary | Objective-focused — rewards durability, OC, mobility for board control |

The other three mission types (Reconnaissance, Priority Assets, Disruption) are **effectively dead** unless GW revises the GT pack.

## Impact on Ranking

When computing rankings without explicit mission flag, use **Purge the Foe** as the default (DPS-weighted). This reflects the real competitive meta.

When `--mission` is specified, honour it, but note in output whether it's a "live" or "dead" mission type.

## If GW Changes Something

This bias expires if:
1. GW releases a new GT Mission Pack with different mission weights
2. A major tournament series (LVO, WCW, etc.) shows a different mission distribution
3. Mordian Glory or equivalent community authority revises the statement

Check `https://www.warhammer-community.com/` for GT pack updates before each major ranking refresh.
