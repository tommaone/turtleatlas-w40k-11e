# Chaos Knights — Disposition & Detachment Tier List

**Source:** 40k.app official 11e database + Goonhammer faction pack review.
**Constraint given:** Actions are bad this edition → Purge and Take and Hold reign.
**Correction note:** Previous Traitoris Lance rank was based on fabricated Sustained Hits/Lethal Hits. Real rule is Paragons of Terror (extra Dread ability). Tier list recalculated with verified data only.

## Layer 1: Disposition Ranking for CK

| Tier | Disposition | Why |
|------|-------------|-----|
| 🟢 S | **Purge the Foe** | CK kills things. Every unit contributes. Natural fit. |
| 🟢 S | **Take and Hold** | With Iconoclast: cheap DAMNED bodies hold objectives. Without: tough Knight bodies still work. |
| 🟡 A | **Priority Assets** | Protect key Knights. OK but CK wants to push, not castle. |
| 🟠 B | **Disruption** | Screening/actions/board control. CK's low model count struggles here. |
| 🔴 C | **Reconnaissance** | Action-heavy. Worst for CK — big bases, few units, can't do actions. |

## Layer 4: Strategic Summary

1. **Best overall army: Houndpack Lance + Iconoclast Fiefdom.** Marked Prey is the strongest detachment rule CK has. Iconoclast fixes objectives. Pick TaH. War Dog spam + cheap bodies wins games.

2. **Best single-box: Infernal Lance** (3DP). Purge disposition. Powerful buffs. If you don't want to run Iconoclast bodies, this is the play.

3. **Best if you own a Tyrant: Traitoris Lance + Bastions of Tyranny.** 3DP total. Purge disposition. Tyrant gets +1 to hit vs BS (situational but free). Traitoris gives extra Dread ability (Doom for +1 to wound vs BS). Stack both buffs: choose Doom + Darkness, Tyrant gets conditional +1 to hit and +1 to wound on battle-shocked targets, plus Stealth. When it works, it hits hard.

4. **Iconoclast Fiefdom is mandatory in competitive lists.** It fixes CK's only real weakness (actions/objectives). Every S or A tier combo includes it.

5. **Helhunt Lance: do not touch.** Worst detachment. Masters of the Pack barely does anything (only Desecrator and Ruinator benefit). Disruption disposition. Not worth 2DP.
